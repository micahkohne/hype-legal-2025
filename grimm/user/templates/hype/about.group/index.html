About

The legal industry is one of the most competitive markets for search, making exposure to target clients a daunting task. Achieving the desired placement for top keywords requires a dedicated strategy that understands the clients’ mindset and online habits.

With a combined twenty-seven years of experience in legal marketing, Hype Legal is a boutique agency dedicated to delivering best-in-class results.

At Hype, we keep it small, focusing on specialized marketing services for an exclusive client list. This approach allows our team to offer a highly individualized and personalized experience for our clients. Our team of designers, developers, and digital marketing specialists are passionate about design and developing a strategy to help your firm grow. Understanding that no two clients are the same, we provide tailored digital marketing services unique to your firm’s individual goals.
